Guardians of the Galaxy

The Guardians of the Galaxy are a band of intergalactic outlaws who teamed together to protect the galaxy from planetary threats. Four years after that, the Guardians joined forces with the Avengers to stop Thanos from collecting the Infinity Stones.

Members

Members

Peter Quill

Peter Jason Quill is a Celestial-Human hybrid who was abducted from Earth by the Yondu Ravager Clan, eventually building a reputation as the notorious intergalactic outlaw Star-Lord.

Rocket

89P13, mainly known as Rocket, is a genetically enhanced creature and a member of the Guardians of the Galaxy.

Gamora

Gamora was a former Zehoberei assassin and a former member of the Guardians of the Galaxy.

Drax

Drax is a former Kylosian intergalactic criminal and a member of the Guardians of the Galaxy.

Groot

Groot is a Flora colossus and the accomplice of Rocket Raccoon.
