ABOUT

The Avengers

The Avengers were a team of extraordinary individuals, with either superpowers or other special characteristics. Though primarily affiliated with the interests of the United States of America, the group's purpose was to protect global stability from inner or extraterrestrial threats. The Avengers were first assembled by S.H.I.E.L.D. as a result of the Avengers Initiative, when Loki invaded Earth with his Chitauri army. The team, consisting of Iron Man, Thor, Captain America, Black Widow, Hawkeye and Hulk, defeated Loki and went their separate ways for a while.

Members

Members

Tony Stark

Anthony Edward "Tony" Stark was a billionaire industrialist, a founding member of the Avengers. A brash but brilliant inventor, his great wealth and exceptional technical knowledge, Stark was one of the world's most powerful men.

Steve Rogers

Captain "Steve" Rogers is a World War II veteran, a founding member of the Avengers, and Earth's first known superhero. Rogers ultimately volunteered for Project Rebirth, where he was the only recipient of the Super Soldier Serum, the serum greatly enhanced Rogers' physical abilities to superhuman levels.

Thor

The serum greatly enhanced Rogers' physical abilities to superhuman levels. Thor was denied the right to become king, While exiled on Earth, Thor learned humility, finding love with Jane Foster, and helped save his new friends from the Destroyer sent by Loki.

Bruce Banner

Doctor Bruce Banner, M.D., Ph.D., is a renowned scientist and a founding member of the Avengers. Highly respected for his work in Gamma Radiation. During the experiment, Banner was exposed to dangerous levels of gamma radiation, in which he can transform himself in powerful beast known as Hulk.

Natasha Romanoff

Natalia Alianovna Romanoff was one of the most talented spies and assassins in the entire world and a founding member of the Avengers. Having extensive mastery in martial arts and armed with her Widow's Bite, Romanoff became one of S.H.I.E.L.D.'s most efficient agents.

Clint Barton

Clinton Francis "Clint" Barton is an extremely skilled marksman, a former special agent of S.H.I.E.L.D. and one of the founding members of the Avengers. Known for his use of the bow and arrow as his primary weapon and an extremely keen eyesight and accuracy that earned him the codename Hawkeye.
