S.H.I.E.L.D.

The Strategic Homeland Intervention, Enforcement, and Logistics Division, better known by its acronym S.H.I.E.L.D., is an American extra-governmental counter-terrorism and intelligence agency, tasked with maintaining both national and global security.

Members

Members

Nick Fury

Former Director of S.H.I.E.L.D. and founder of the Avengers.

Phil Coulson

A highly decorated agent and former Director of S.H.I.E.L.D.. Coulson was Nick Fury's right-hand man for many important missions and his liaison to the Avengers.

Maria Hill

Former Deputy Director of S.H.I.E.L.D., and also held the title of "Commander".
